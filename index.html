Akan Name Engine.

The Akan Name Engine is a web_application that derives a Ghanian Akan name for the user.Akanian naming is a Ghanian culture that emphasizes on

giving chlidren their first name as a "day name" whichcorresponds to the day in the week they were born.

To do this the user has to input his or her birthday and and the gender, then Akan Name Engine calculates the day of the they were born and then

depending on their gender it outputs their Akan Name.To enjoy this services you have to input your gender and birthday below.
