Akan_Name_Engine.io



The Akan Name Engine is a web_application that derives a Ghanian Akan name for the user.Akanian naming is a Ghanian culture that emphasizes

on giving chlidren their first name as a "day name" which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. To do this the user has to input his or

her birthday and and the gender, then Akan Name Engine calculates the day of the they were born and then depending on their gender it outputs

their Akan Name.To enjoy this services you have to input your gender and birthday below.Click the Nyagah-Tech logo to refresh the form.
